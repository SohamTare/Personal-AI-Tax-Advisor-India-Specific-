Deep Dive into Tax-Saving Options

National Pension System (NPS)

Key features of NPS contributions:

  • An additional deduction of up to ₹50,000 is available under Section 80CCD(1B).
  • This is over and above the ₹1.5 lakh limit of Section 80C.

Education Loan Interest (Section 80E)

Deduction for interest on education loans:

  • You can claim a deduction on the full interest paid on an education loan.
  • This deduction is for a maximum of 8 years.
  • There is no upper limit on the interest amount that can be deducted.

Rent Deduction (Section 80GG)

Conditions for claiming rent deduction:

  • This is for individuals who do not receive HRA as part of their salary.
  • The deduction can be up to ₹60,000 per year.
  • You must not own a residential property in the city where you work.

Savings Account Interest (Section 80TTA)

Explanation of the deduction for savings account interest:

  • A deduction of up to ₹10,000 is available on interest earned from savings accounts.
  • This deduction is applicable to all individuals and HUFs.

Charitable Donations (Section 80G)

Overview of how donations can save tax:

  • Donations made to certain approved funds and institutions are eligible for deduction.
  • The deduction amount can be either 50% or 100% of the donated amount, depending on the recipient.

Health Insurance Premium (Section 80D)

Claim deductions for health insurance premiums paid for your family:

  • Up to ₹25,000 for self, spouse, and children.
  • Additional ₹25,000 for parents (or ₹50,000 if they are senior citizens).

Investments & Expenses (Section 80C)

The most popular tax-saving section, with a deduction limit of ₹1.5 lakhs. It includes:

  • Life Insurance premiums.
  • Provident Fund (EPF/PPF) contributions.
  • Equity Linked Savings Schemes (ELSS).
  • Children's tuition fees.

Interest on Deposits (80TTA & 80TTB)

Save tax on interest earned from bank deposits:

  • *80TTA:* Up to ₹10,000 deduction on savings account interest for all individuals.
  • *80TTB:* Up to ₹50,000 deduction on all bank interest for senior citizens.
